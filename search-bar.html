About Us


My Food Direct is a company that provides customers fresh food and agricultural products with home delivery services.
what do we do
We promote the selling of agriculture products and providing home delivery service for our customers.
Our Mission
... We take action for Goal 2 of Sustainable Development Goals - Zero Hunger
Support Local Farmers
We support our local farmers by buying food products from small-scale local farmers and selling their products to our consumers through our platform.
This helps to increase agricultural productivity and their income.
Support KidsCan Charitable Trust
KidsCan is now helping feed more than 40,000 New Zealand kids everyday.
We will donate 5% of our profit to the KidsCan Charitable Trust.
We ensure that a portion of our profit received is donated to those in need of food.
We also allow our visitors to make donations for KidsCan Charitable Trust through our website.
